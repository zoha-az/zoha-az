## Research Projects

### GAIA — Intelligent Climate-Friendly Video Platform

**Funder:** Austrian Research Promotion Agency (FFG) · Grant No. FO99989784

**Duration:** September 2023 – September 2024

**My Role:** Working on AI-assisted solutions to improve energy efficiency in video streaming, including machine learning-based encoding optimization and sustainable adaptive streaming pipelines.

GAIA aims to develop a next-generation video platform that integrates artificial intelligence to minimize energy consumption throughout the video streaming lifecycle — from encoding and transcoding to delivery and playback.

---

### ATHENA — Adaptive Streaming over HTTP and Emerging Networked Multimedia Services

**Funder:** Christian Doppler Research Association (CDG)

**Duration:** October 2019 – February 2023

**My Role:** Working on AI-assisted solutions to improve energy efficiency in video streaming within the context of network-assisted and edge-cloud video delivery systems.

ATHENA focuses on building and validating frameworks for network-assisted adaptive video delivery, leveraging software-defined networking (SDN), network function virtualization (NFV), and computing resources distributed across edge and cloud infrastructure.
