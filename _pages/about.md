Hi there! I am Zoha Azimi and I am a Ph.D. researcher and university assistant at the Institute of Information Technology (ITEC), Universität Klagenfurt, focusing on AI-assisted, energy-efficient video streaming. My work explores the integration of large language models (LLMs) and machine learning to optimize video processing and streaming sustainability. Previously, I worked in AI research and development at Maps Group in Italy, designing deep learning models for energy forecasting. I also hold two master’s degrees, one in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Bologna and another in Electrical Engineering (Telecommunications) from IUST, Iran, where I gained experience in deep learning, computer vision, and signal processing.
