Hi! I am **Zoha Azimi**, a PhD researcher and University Assistant at the [Institute of Information Technology (ITEC)](https://www.aau.at/itec/), Universität Klagenfurt, Austria.

My research focuses on **AI-assisted, energy-efficient video streaming** — exploring how large language models (LLMs) and machine learning can optimize video processing while reducing energy consumption and improving sustainability. I am supervised by [Prof. Christian Timmerer](https://timmerer.com/) and [Prof. Radu Prodan](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=QfyMFnIAAAAJ), and advised by [Dr. Reza Farahani](https://rezafar.github.io/).

## Research Interests
- Large Language Models (LLMs) for multimedia systems
- Edge AI and distributed computing
- Energy-efficient video streaming and encoding
- Deep learning for computer vision and signal processing
